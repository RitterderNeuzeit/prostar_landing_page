## Copywriting Guidelines

### Tone & Voice by Variant

**Warm-Minimal:**

- Professional yet approachable
- Educational and empowering
- Focus on practical results
- Uses "Sie" (formal German)
- Balances expertise with accessibility

**Sachlich-Professional:**

- Corporate and authoritative
- Data-driven and results-oriented
- Focus on ROI and metrics
- Uses "Sie" (formal German)
- Emphasizes credibility and proven results

**Handwerklich-Authentisch:**

- Personal and conversational
- Relatable and encouraging
- Focus on authenticity and community
- Uses "Du" (informal German)
- Emphasizes personal transformation

### Key Message Pillars

Across all variants, ensure these core messages are present:

1. **Integrity**: Honest, transparent approach to Social Media
2. **Loyalty**: Building genuine community, not just followers
3. **Courage**: Taking bold action with authentic messaging
4. **Engagement**: Creating meaningful interactions and relationships

### CTA Hierarchy

**Primary CTAs** (Highest Conversion Intent):

- Kostenlose Mini-Lektion herunterladen
- Kurs buchen
- Jetzt starten

**Secondary CTAs** (Information Gathering):

- Mehr erfahren
- Kostenlose Beratung

**Tertiary CTAs** (Commitment):

- Jetzt Anmelden
- Kontakt

### Conversion Optimization Tips

1. **Use specific numbers** (‚Ç¨97, +250%, 5 Modules) rather than vague claims
2. **Include social proof** with names, companies, and specific results
3. **Address objections** in FAQ section
4. **Create urgency** with limited-time offers or scarcity (if applicable)
5. **Use benefit-driven language** ("Messbare Ergebnisse" vs. "Gute Ergebnisse")
6. **Include trust signals** (30-day money-back guarantee, lifetime access)
7. **Personalize messaging** based on audience segment

## Accessibility & Inclusivity

All copy should:

- Use clear, simple language
- Avoid jargon or explain technical terms
- Provide sufficient contrast between text and background
- Include alt text for all images
- Use descriptive link text (not "click here")
